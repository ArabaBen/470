Living together

Studies suggest that children raised in multigenerational households within a typical Eastern and ethnic society had far more favorable upbrings than that of children raised in a typical American nuclear family. This means raising children in homes where grandparents, your siblings, aunts/uncles/cousins etc. are present under one roof provides necessary support for children to develop and hit crucial milestones without setbacks.
But we get it, living with extended family can be complicated and at times difficult. But there's so many benefits to be reaped that may outweigh the drawbacks.
Families and their structures are as complex as we are human. There isn't a one size fits all when it comes to family choices. Nuclear, multi, communila or somewhere in between- we've got you covered on the how tos. That's why we created this resources to help you choice styles and practices that suits your needs & family creation.
