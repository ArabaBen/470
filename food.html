Mealtime Tips

when it comes to meal time, all hands on deck with less distractions.

Place all phones in a bowl away from the dining room.

Clear all eating space to allow for room. The more space, the more transparent and honest we can all be.

ask for roses and thorns. give compliments for big & little things.

It’s the little things and moments that count. Simply making everyone feel heard and full brings more togetherness during mealtime.
